## 2024-05-22 - [CLI Feedback Enhancement]
**Learning:** For production-grade SDKs and CLI tools, users often perform long-running batch operations or complex analyses. Providing immediate, visual progress feedback and human-readable summaries (using emojis and units) significantly reduces perceived latency and makes the tool feel more responsive and polished than raw dictionary output.
**Action:** Always favor structured, formatted terminal output with status indicators for any command that processes data or performs multi-step validation.
